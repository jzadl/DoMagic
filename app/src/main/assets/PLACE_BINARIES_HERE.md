# Required Binaries

Place the binaries extracted from the Magisk APK here:

## arm64-v8a/
- magiskboot   (from lib/arm64-v8a/libmagiskboot.so)
- magiskinit   (from lib/arm64-v8a/libmagiskinit.so)
- init-ld      (from lib/arm64-v8a/libinit-ld.so, if it exists)

## x86_64/
- magiskboot   (from lib/x86_64/libmagiskboot.so)
- magiskinit   (from lib/x86_64/libmagiskinit.so)

## fastboot/arm64-v8a/
- fastboot     (static ARM64 binary, see main README)

## stub.apk
- From the Magisk APK: assets/stub.apk

Download Magisk APK from:
https://github.com/topjohnwu/Magisk/releases/latest
